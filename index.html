about me

I am a computational social scientist dedicated to understanding health inequalities with advanced AI algorithms.

I specialise in the study of health disparities in mortality and morbidity, leveraging machine learning, deep learning algorithms, and explainable Artificial Intelligence. My particular emphasis is on health-related issues concerning the aging population, and I'm keen on unraveling the influence of social determinants on health outcomes to expose health inequalities. This understanding assists in proposing prevention-oriented policies, thereby bridging the health inequality gap.
